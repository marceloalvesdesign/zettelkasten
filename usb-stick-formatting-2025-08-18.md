1. Click the Apple Icon at the top left corner of your screen.
2. Click "About this Mac"
3. Click "More Info..."
4. Click "System Report..."
5. Go to `Hardware > USB`.

Your device should be listed there.

## Formatting

1. Open "Disk Utility".
2. If you don't see your USB drive, go to `View > Show All Devices`.
3. Erase to format. Pick `ExtFat` if you want compatibility between Windows as MacOS.
